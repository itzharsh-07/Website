Est. 2002

Two Decades of Craft

Lumière began in a single workshop dedicated to one idea: furniture should be made to outlive trends, and to be handed down.

Our Philosophy

Built for the Long Term

Every Lumière piece begins as a sketch, refined over months with our in-house design studio and a network of master artisans across Italy, Denmark and the American Southeast. We work only in solid woods, full-grain leathers, hand-honed marble, and metals cast to last — never veneers, never particleboard. The result is furniture that ages with dignity: patinas deepen, leathers soften, and grain grows richer with every year of use.

Today, over 120 artisans contribute to the Lumière collection, and more than 58,000 homes around the world live with pieces we've made. We remain, as we always have, a company obsessed with the details no one else notices — until they live with them.

Our Principles

Material Integrity

Solid woods, full-grain leathers, and natural stone — sourced responsibly, never substituted.

Slow Craftsmanship

Each collection takes 12–18 months from sketch to showroom — we don't rush what's meant to last.

Quiet Design

We design for the room, not the trend cycle — pieces that feel as considered in ten years as they do today.
