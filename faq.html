Frequently Asked Questions

Standard delivery arrives in 2–4 weeks; Express delivery arrives in 5–7 business days. Every order includes white-glove delivery as an option at checkout, where our team unpacks and places your furniture for you.

We work exclusively in solid hardwoods, full-grain leathers, natural marble and stone, and cast or forged metals — never particleboard, veneer, or bonded leather.

You may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided they are in original condition. See our Shipping & Returns page for full details.

Every Lumière piece is covered by a 10-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects in materials and construction.

Select collections offer alternate finishes and upholstery. Reach out to our design concierge team via the Contact page for custom quotes.

We currently ship within the United States and Canada. International shipping is available on request for select collections — contact our concierge team for a quote.

Each order ships with a care guide specific to its materials. In general, keep wood pieces out of direct sunlight and condition leather every 6–12 months.
