Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Standard delivery arrives within 2–4 weeks of order confirmation; Express delivery arrives in 5–7 business days for an additional $89. Orders over $1,500 ship free.

White-glove delivery is available at checkout on all large furniture items — our delivery partners will unpack, assemble, and place your furniture, then remove all packaging.

Returns

We want you to love your Lumière piece. If it isn't right, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund to your original payment method.

  • Items must be in original, unused condition with all original packaging where possible.
  • Custom and made-to-order pieces are final sale.
  • Return shipping is complimentary for defective or damaged items; a return logistics fee may apply otherwise.

How to Start a Return

Contact our concierge team at concierge@lumiere.example or call +1 (800) 555-0142 with your order number, and we'll coordinate pickup.

Damaged or Defective Items

Please inspect your delivery on arrival. Report any damage within 48 hours so we can arrange a free replacement or repair.
